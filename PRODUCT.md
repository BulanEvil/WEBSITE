# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Evergold serves global B2B buyers who need reliable supply for industrial equipment parts. Primary audiences include overseas distributors, repair workshops, end equipment users, procurement companies, mining operators, and engineering contractors.

These visitors are usually evaluating fit, trust, response speed, and export reliability. They may arrive with part numbers, equipment models, engine models, or broad category needs, and they need a clear path to request a quote.

## Product Purpose

The website presents Evergold as a professional international supplier for construction machinery parts, diesel engine parts, mining equipment parts, and generator sets.

Success means visitors can quickly understand what Evergold supplies, judge whether the company is credible, and contact sales with enough confidence to send an RFQ.

## Brand Personality

Professional, international, and dependable.

The tone should feel practical and trade-ready: direct, calm, precise, and useful. It should avoid over-selling and instead build confidence through clarity, product relevance, and visible contact paths.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI landing pages with purple gradients, glass panels, and vague slogans.
- Decorative industrial pages that look dramatic but hide the product categories.
- Crowded catalog pages that feel like a parts dump without hierarchy.
- Consumer luxury styling, editorial fashion layouts, or playful startup aesthetics.
- Over-animated pages that slow down global access or distract from RFQ actions.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with trade clarity. The first screen should immediately show what Evergold supplies and how to contact sales.
2. Make trust visible. Use structured product categories, export language, stable contact information, and practical proof points.
3. Design for scanning. Overseas buyers should be able to scan categories, capabilities, and quote paths quickly.
4. Keep the interface globally accessible. Pages should be fast, responsive, readable, and light enough for varied network conditions.
5. Use restrained industrial confidence. The site should feel serious and capable without becoming dark, noisy, or heavy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for color contrast, keyboard navigation, visible focus states, readable text sizing, and reduced-motion-safe interactions.

The site should be easy to use for non-native English speakers. Copy should be clear, concrete, and short. Avoid idioms, overly poetic headlines, and decorative text that makes product meaning harder to understand.
